What We Do

AIVO Development Limited is a UK-registered software development company focused on building proprietary digital products for Apple and web platforms. We design, engineer, and maintain our applications in-house, from initial concept through release and long-term support.

Our products are developed with a strong emphasis on reliability, security, and user experience. We follow structured engineering practices, documented workflows, and formal testing procedures to ensure that every release performs consistently in real-world conditions.

We operate as a product-driven company rather than a consultancy. Our focus is the creation and continuous improvement of our own software applications, built to serve professional and general users across global markets.

Product Focus

Our current development programme centres on specialised digital applications for iOS, macOS, and web environments. These include productivity and performance-oriented tools designed for structured use, measurable outcomes, and long-term maintainability.

We build natively for Apple platforms, adhering to Apple’s Human Interface Guidelines, platform integration standards, and privacy requirements. Our software is engineered to meet the technical and policy expectations of the App Store review process.

All products are designed for international distribution, with scalable architecture and compliance considerations incorporated from the outset of development.

Our Approach

We apply a quality-first development methodology. Each feature is implemented, reviewed, and tested before inclusion in production builds. Code clarity, maintainability, and performance optimisation are core engineering priorities.

Privacy-conscious design is foundational to our systems. We collect only data that is strictly necessary for functionality, and we design our infrastructure to minimise data exposure and operational risk.

Our architectural decisions prioritise long-term sustainability. We favour structured, well-documented systems that can evolve as products scale and as our internal engineering team expands.
